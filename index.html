About

Eliot Cotton —
practitioner and educator.

Eliot Cotton is a senior legal advisor and external counsel with deep experience in private equity fund governance, complex transactions, and management company matters.

He is also a professor at the University of Texas School of Law, where he teaches Negotiation for Transactional Lawyers — a practice-based course built around simulation, frameworks, and the craft of real-world deal-making.
